About Us

A few words...

Today we learn from anything and everything — the books we find, articles on the web, other humans, places we visit — we’re learning all the time. Which is why we started this project by collecting tens of thousands of articles, videos and classes over the last few months.

As a kid reading wasn’t “cool”, we think it should be. We live in a world where internal growth has been left on the sidelines of our curriculums, we think understanding ourselves is pretty important in the grand scheme of things.

So far we've built half a product with a lot of TLC, we're hoping to build the second half with you. If you share in our belief that every man and woman should hope for an endless education, an interesting job and a fulfilling life... Atlas is for you.

Our mission is to inspire curiosity and prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow.
