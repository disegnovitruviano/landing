About Undermind

Our Beliefs

Hi there, I’m Tom. I want to share a few words about the beliefs and experiences that led us to build Undermind.

We’re a curious bunch and we love learning so much that we use all our free time trying to master new skills. It’s a shame we weren’t as excited about school. Over the last few months we collected tens of thousands of articles, videos and classes, because we know that today people learn from anything and everything — the books we find, articles on the web, other humans, places we visit —we're learning all the time. We share a passion for games and therefore game-based learning. We couldn’t imagine Undermind as anything other than a game, each and every new piece fitting nicely into our brain puzzles with a reward to help us finish the picture. Humans are complicated and we need to start accepting that there is no such thing as a "one-size-fits-all" magic recipe for teaching. We asked ourselves “what's better than a magic recipe?” and dreamt up a lifelong learning wizard for ourselves, Anorak. In his infinite wisdom (and sometimes humour) he can slowly begin to understand our needs, interests, abilities, dreams, and help us reach our full potential. As millennials we’re happy to abide by our generational standards: we disagree with unwelcome authority, and we particularly dislike the status quo of schools telling us what to think, what to do and what to say. If the 21st century doesn't fit neatly into rows, neither should we. As a kid reading wasn’t “cool”, but it should be. We live in a world where somehow internal growth and fulfilment have been left on the sidelines. Our own mind is an ocean of complexity that builds our entire reality, understanding ourselves seems pretty important in the grand scheme of things. For all the reasons above, and many more, we teamed up and built Undermind on our weekends, mornings and evenings. We built half a product with a lot of TLC, and we want to build the other half with you. If you believe that every man and woman should hope for a never-ending education, an interesting job and a fulfilling life, then Undermind is for you. Our mission is to transform education, inspire curiosity and prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow. Happy learning! Tom BTW: I’m Chroniclr on Wordpress or tom@undermind.io via email.
