About Undermind

We're millennials. We disagree with unwelcome authority, and we're not fond of the status quo: schools telling us what to think, what to do and what to say. If the 21st century doesn't fit neatly into rows, neither should we.

As a kid reading wasn’t “cool”, but it should be. We started by building half a product with a lot of TLC, and we're hoping to build the second half with you. If you believe that every man and woman should hope for an endless education, an interesting job and a fulfilling life... Undermind is for you.

Our mission is to transform education, inspire curiosity and prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow.
