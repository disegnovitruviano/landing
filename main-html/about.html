About Undermind

Our mission is to transform education, inspire curiosity and prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Beliefs

There is no single way to teach, no magic recipe. Every human being is different. Everyone has different needs, interests, abilities, goals, and environments. There isn’t only one way to live our live, one way to do our jobs, why should there be one way to learn? For over a hundred years schools have told us what to think, what to do, and what to say. The 21st century does not fit neatly into rows, and neither will we.

Today we can end the era of mass education by harnessing new technologies to make learning as unique as each individual. Undermind empowers students to think for themselves, to learn at their own pace and to make their own decisions. Today we learn from anything and everything — books we find, articles, other humans, places we visit — there are no divisions between learning and life, we're learning all the time.

Our education should teach us to become lifelong learners, which is why Undermind flows and changes with us.

We live in a world where the subjects that focus on our internal growth and fulfilment have been left on the sidelines of our curriculums. The human mind is an ocean of complexity that builds every element of our reality, understanding ourselves must become a priority. Our minds are all we have.

We believe the purpose of education is to make it possible for every single person to achieve, not only the skills and qualifications needed to meet the challenges of today’s world, but also the harmonious development of their humanity. It is our hope that someday in the near future, every man and woman will hope for a job that is interesting and fulfilling, every man and woman will seek personal fulfillment and happiness.
